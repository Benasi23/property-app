## Brand basics

**Colours** (Design → Site Styles → Colours):
- Deep slate (primary dark / hero / footer): `#27323B`
- Darker slate (depth): `#222F39`
- Antique gold / bronze (buttons, accents, headings): `#C79D6F`
- Off-white (text on dark): `#F4F1EC`
- White (light sections): `#FFFFFF`

**Fonts** (Design → Fonts): Headings = **Cormorant Garamond** or **Marcellus** (refined
serif). Body = **Montserrat** or **Inter** (clean sans). Buttons/labels in the sans.

**Logo:** use `moneta-group-logo.png`. On dark sections use it as-is; you can also upload
a gold-on-transparent version for the header if you have one.

**Tone:** premium, confident, understated. Short sentences. No hype words ("best", "#1").

## IMAGES — cinematic, corporate, Australian property

Best option: use **your own** project renders/photography if you have them — nothing beats
real stock.

Otherwise use royalty-free (free for commercial use, no attribution needed) from
**Unsplash.com** or **Pexels.com**. Search terms that match the look:
- "modern australian house twilight" / "house and land estate australia"
- "australian suburban home facade dusk"
- "brisbane melbourne skyline aerial" (for regional/credibility sections)
- "modern home interior australia" / "new build handover keys"
- "aerial new housing estate" (cinematic masterplan feel)

Shot direction: twilight/golden-hour exteriors, warm lights on, wide cinematic framing,
muted tones that sit well with slate + gold. Avoid bright stocky/cheesy images. Apply a
subtle dark overlay on hero images so the gold text and logo stay legible.

Tip: keep one consistent treatment (e.g. slightly desaturated, warm) across all photos so
the site feels considered, not stock.

## PORTAL LOGIN — the link into your platform

Portal Login is just a link to the platform we built.

- **Header button:** Edit → click the header → enable the built-in **Button** → text:
  "Partner Login" → Link: your platform URL → toggle **Open in new window**.
- **In-page buttons:** add a **Button block**, same link, same "open in new window".
- **The URL:** while the platform runs on your computer it's `http://localhost:3000/login`
  (only works on your machine). Once it's deployed (e.g. Vercel) it becomes something like
  `https://app.monetagroup.com.au/login`. Set it once now and update after deployment.

That's the whole integration — the website is the front door, the button hands approved
partners into the live platform.
